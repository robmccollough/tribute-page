Dr. William Mark McCollough

World Class Oncologist, Dedicated Environmentalist, Loving Father

Dr. McCollough is shown here with his son Robert, and beautiful wife Robin.

Work

Dr. McCollough has almost 35 years of experience working in the field of radiation oncology. Graduating from University of Florida Medical School in 1985 as one of the best in his graduating class, he completed his residency at Shands Hospital at the Universiy of Florida in 1989. He began his career as a fully fledged radiation oncologist at Phoebe Hospital in Albany, GA. Since then he has worked at Pardee Hospital in Hendersonville, NC, and is currently one of the leading cancer specialists at Mission Hospital in Asheville, NC.

His committment to the people he treats is inspiring and speaks for itself. He has been named one of North Carolina's Best Doctors over 20 years in a row by Business North Carolina.

Environmental Ventures

Outside of his practice, Dr. McCollough is a champion of enviromental preservation and climate change awareness. He owns a solar farm in St. Louis, MO that provides clean renewable energy to surrounding farms, doing his part to reduce reliance on coal energy in the farming industry.

In addition, Dr. McCollough donates routinely to many environmental-preservation-oriented charities based in and around Asheville. He would volunteer himself but hes too busy literally saving peoples lives.

Dr. McCollough is also very concious of his and his families carbon footprint. He maintains complete control over the thermostats in his home, and has a sixth sense for when lights are on when no one is in the room. Dr. McCollough also drives a 100% electric car, doing his part to reduce reliance on fossil fuels.

Life

Dr. McCollough was born in 1959 and grew up mostly in Gainesville, FL. He is the oldest of 4 siblings and has 3 sisters. He attended Davidson College for 2 years in 1978, where he was a member of Phi Gamma Alpha Fraternity. He transferred to UF in 1981 with a 4.0 GPA.

After graduating medical school 1989, while attending a party, he met a law student named Robin Adams. 3 months later they were married. Together they spent some years travelling the world, before moving to Albany GA where Dr. McCollough practiced at Pardee Hospital. By 2002 they had 3 beautiful children; Madison, Robert, and Thomas. Mark missed the mountains of North Carolina where he visited as a youth, and the family moved to Brevard NC in 2003, and again to Asheville NC in 2006. During this time Mark earned his reputation as one of the best cancer specialists in the state.

Dr. McCollough is an avid outdoorsman on the weekends. He loves camping, hiking, and especially cycling. Mark onced placed 1st in a comptetive 100mile road race, and has been invited to compete in numerous races annually including the Assault on Mount Mitchell. Always a gator fan, his love for the swamp has never lessened, but now he roots for the Tar Heels as well. He also enjoys playing basketball, football, and ping pong. Always made more fun if his kids are in the mix.
